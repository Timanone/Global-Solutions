1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to ensure proper website functionality, improve user experience, and enable communication features.

2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies and similar technologies strictly for functional and operational purposes, including:

  • Ensuring proper website operation and security
  • Enabling communication tools and inquiry submission
  • Preventing abuse and technical misuse

We do not use cookies for automated profiling or behavioral advertising.

3. Types of Cookies We Use

a) Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function correctly and cannot be disabled through our systems. They include cookies related to:

  • Website security
  • Session management
  • Technical stability

b) Functional Cookies

Functional cookies enable specific features requested by the user, such as communication and inquiry tools.

These may include cookies set by third-party service providers used for contact or inquiry handling (e.g. embedded communication widgets).

c) Analytics Cookies (Future Use)

At this time, we do not actively use analytics or tracking cookies.

However, analytics tools may be introduced in the future to better understand website usage and improve functionality.

If such tools are implemented, this Cookie Policy will be updated accordingly and, where required by law, user consent will be obtained.

4. Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies may be set by third-party service providers that support website functionality, such as communication or inquiry handling tools.

These providers process data in accordance with their own privacy policies and applicable data protection laws.

Such providers may include communication and inquiry handling tools used on this website.

5. Managing Cookies

You can manage or delete cookies at any time through your browser settings.

Most browsers allow you to:

  • View stored cookies
  • Delete existing cookies
  • Block cookies entirely
  • Receive a warning before cookies are stored

Please note that disabling certain cookies may affect website functionality.

6. Changes to This Cookie Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in technology, legal requirements, or website functionality.

Any updates will be published on this page with a revised date.
